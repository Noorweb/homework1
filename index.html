Tour in Riyadh

It is the largest city on the Arabian Peninsula Riyadh, and is situated in the center of the an-Nafud desert, on the eastern part of the Najd plateau. The city sits at an average of 600 meters (2,000 ft) above sea level,[6] and receives around 5 million tourists each year, making it the forty-ninth most visited city in the world and the 6th in the Middle East.

Riyadh had a population of 7.0 million people in 2022, making it the most-populous city in Saudi Arabia, 3rd most populous in the Middle East, and the 38th most populous in Asia.
